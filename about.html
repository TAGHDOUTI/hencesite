About Us


HENCEFORTH is a non-profit research and development (R&D) center in advanced information technology. HENCEFORTH is a specialized center focused on pushing the boundaries of technological innovation and creating cutting-edge solutions in the field of Information Technology in the digital era.

HENCEFORTH brings together a team of highly skilled professionals, including researchers, experts, engineers, and scientists, who work collaboratively to drive advancements in various areas of IT and address the evolving needs of businesses and society. The HENCEFORTH mix of research and development allows us to create critical mass in various specialties to tackle the challenges of the new digital world.
